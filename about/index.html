About me

IAN, a pilot, once was a programmer.
An excellent programmer among pilots, and an excellent pilot among programmers.

I like simplicity, so I recently redesigned this website with a minimalist style and no unnecessary interactions. I’m pretty sure even fewer people will visit this page now — and that’s perfectly fine.

I originally built this site to document my technical growth, but over the past few years, I haven’t spent much time on it. Then I changed careers and became a pilot, which left me with even less time to write.

This redesign feels like a fresh start — a way to document more of my journey, not just in technology, but in life.

I believe life is measured not by time, but by experiences. That’s why I’m always exploring, always learning, and always searching for something new. I look forward to becoming a better version of myself.

What about you, my friend?
