Writing in front of posts

Writing in front of posts

Writing some words in front of all.

In fact, this is my first technical blog, and also my first blog written in English.

At this stage of my life, I’ve come to realize how important English is. Unfortunately, I didn’t make the most of the chance to learn it during my school years. Now, I’ve started learning it again, although I’m not sure whether it’s too late or whether it will make much difference. Perhaps that’s also one of the reasons why I choose to write this blog in English.

Time always seems to pass too quickly.

Back in 2014, I often asked myself when I would finally graduate and step into the real world. 2018 felt so far away then — almost unreachable. But in the blink of an eye, four years were gone.

And now, looking back, I sometimes feel like I’m still the same person I was four years ago — no remarkable achievements, no wealth, nothing worth showing off.

Ha, maybe I’ve gone a little too far with that thought.

To be honest, I’m someone who tends to worry too much, often fearing more than the danger itself.

Still, I believe in staying quiet, working hard, and growing in silence.

Have a good journey.
